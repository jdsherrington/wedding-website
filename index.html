FAQ

What is the dress code?

The dress code is semi-formal or smart casual. The fancier the better!

Will alcohol be served on premise?

The wedding reception will be a dry event to fit with the more relaxed and intimate atmosphere of the day, however we will be well-stocked with a wide range of beverages.

Can I bring my children?

Absolutely! We're also going to have a few different lawn games available at the venue, so there will be fun to be had for all ages.

Is it okay to give cash instead of an item from the registry?

We feel extraordinarily blessed for anything that anyone decides to gift us, our only desire is that you give whatever you feel led to give. Most of all we hope you'll gift us with your presence on our special day to celebrate with us!

I have special dietary considerations, will there be options for me?

Please include any dietary requirements as part of your RSVP below, this will allow us to communicate numbers to our caterers and make sure that everyone's needs are met. We won't let you go hungry!

What's the best place to find photos of the wedding after the event?

We will be updating this website to display all of the photos taken, until the website hosting expires early next year. However we will also endeavour to make as many photos publicly available through our social media feeds as well.
